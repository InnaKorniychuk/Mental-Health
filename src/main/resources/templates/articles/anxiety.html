Anxiety: how can you cope with it?

What causes anxiety?

Anxiety often arises as a response to uncertainty or fear of the future. It can have both physical and psychological symptoms.

How can you reduce anxiety?

  • Breathing exercises and grounding techniques.
  • Limiting time spent on social media and news.
  • Keeping an emotions journal.
  • Spending time outdoors.
  • Consulting a psychologist.

Advice from a psychologist:

Do not avoid anxiety — acknowledge it, but do not let it control you. It will pass if you remain calm and offer yourself support.
