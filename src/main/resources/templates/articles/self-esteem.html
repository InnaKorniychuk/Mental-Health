Self-Esteem & Self-Acceptance

What is self-esteem?

Self-esteem is a person’s perception of their own worth. It develops from childhood and continually evolves with experience.

Steps towards self-acceptance:

  • Don’t compare yourself to others. You are unique.
  • Replace self-criticism with self-support. Change your inner dialogue.
  • Accept your emotions. Don’t be afraid to feel sadness or fear.
  • Take small steps towards your goals. Every step is already an achievement.
  • Record your accomplishments. Even the smallest ones!

Psychologist’s advice:

Self-acceptance doesn’t mean stopping your growth. It’s about loving yourself today while being ready to become an even better version of yourself tomorrow.
