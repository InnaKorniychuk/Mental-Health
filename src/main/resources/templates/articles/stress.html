How to Manage Stress

Why do we feel stress?

Stress is the body’s natural response to challenging or new situations. It can be short-term or chronic if no steps are taken to reduce it.

Effective ways to reduce stress:

  • Deep breathing: inhale slowly through your nose for a count of four, hold your breath, and exhale over four counts.
  • Physical activity: daily walks, yoga, or exercise help lower cortisol levels.
  • Meditation: mindfulness practices help you focus on the present moment.
  • Talk to someone: share your feelings with a friend or professional.
  • “3-3-3” technique: name three things you can see, hear, and touch to ground yourself.

Psychologist’s advice:

Learn to recognise the early signs of stress and don’t neglect rest. Self-care is not a luxury, it’s a necessity.
